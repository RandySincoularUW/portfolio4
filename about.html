About Me

I am excited to be a part of the new online Master of Science program: GIS & Web Map Programming, at UW-Madison. This will give me the opportunity to learn the latest GIS software and advance my web based GIS developments skills.

My background in GIS includes working at an Electric & Gas utility, the Department of Transportation, and City of Milwaukee. I have over 20 years of experience using and supporting Esri software such as ArcMap and more recently ArcGIS Server and Portal for ArcGIS.

I am involved in developing client server applications and providing troubleshooting and support of our GIS applications. I also have a lot of experience working with multiple workgroups while coordinating complex technical projects such as software upgrades and implementing new applications that interface with GIS.

I am always looking for new ways to use GIS and spatial technology to solve problems and make it easier for people to do their jobs.
